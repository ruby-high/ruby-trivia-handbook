## ✅ Just shipped
- **We're in the Google Play Store.** Live listing — free, no ads, **no account required**, kid-safe. Same game as the web, just packaged, and verified crypto-free for the store edition. Amazon's in the same review queue; Steam's next.
- **Five new games — live.** Chess, Checkers, Sudoku, Hangman, and Math Sprint, all running on the same server engine as live multiplayer. Ruby's a **games hub** now, not just a quiz.
- **The daily X poll reads properly again.** Long answers were getting cut off mid-word, so two options could look identical — impossible to vote on. Answers now stay short and readable, and the poll only picks questions that fit.
- **The 24/7 channel got steadier.** The always-on stream no longer freezes on a network hiccup — it reconnects itself instead of needing a refresh. The scan-to-play QR always stays on screen, question difficulty is shown, and long questions get extra reading time.
- **Question quality, on autopilot.** A nightly pass tidies up questions flagged as too wordy, so they get fixed instead of sitting in the deck.
