## ✅ Just shipped
- **Both store builds ready and going in.** Google Play and Amazon, from the same code. Free, no ads, and **no account required** — open it and play. Signed, size-slimmed, and verified crypto-free for the store editions.
- **The daily X poll reads properly again.** Long answers were getting cut off mid-word, so two different options could end up looking identical — impossible to vote on. Answers now stay short and readable, and the poll only picks questions that actually fit.
- **The 24/7 channel got steadier.** The always-on stream no longer freezes on a network hiccup — it reconnects by itself instead of needing a refresh. The scan-to-play QR now always stays on screen, question difficulty is shown, and long questions get extra reading time.
- **Question quality, on autopilot.** A nightly pass now tidies up questions flagged as too wordy, so they get fixed instead of sitting in the deck.

## What we're focused on
- **Store reviews.** Google Play and Amazon both in the queue — free, no ads, same game everywhere. Steam still on deck.
- **The live channel.** Keep the always-on / play-along stream running, and make it a regular thing.
- **Holder perks.** The web3 recognition layer — holder badge, holders-only leaderboard, cosmetics — recognition-first. Connect at [web3.ruby-trivia.com](https://web3.ruby-trivia.com).
- **A new game, early days.** Ruby is learning to write **mad libs** — fill-in-the-blank stories meant to be played out loud together. The writing engine is built; the game itself hasn't been started, so no dates yet.
- **Keep it solid.** Stability, multiplayer polish, more questions.
- **On the horizon:** one Ruby account that links everything — web, mobile, Telegram, X, wallet — so there's no more juggling scattered logins.
