# Roadmap

Where Ruby Trivia's headed. Utility-first — **targets, not guarantees.**

## Now
- **Live &amp; free** — daily trivia + live multiplayer at [ruby-trivia.com](https://ruby-trivia.com).
- **Going multi-platform** — Amazon Appstore &amp; Steam builds submitting. Same game, no ads, everywhere.
- **Holder perks** rolling out — recognition-first. → [details](web3)

## Next
- **Solid &amp; fun** — stability, multiplayer polish, more &amp; better questions.
- **One Ruby account** — links web, mobile, Telegram, X, wallet. No more scattered logins.
- **More ways to play** — new game modes beyond trivia.

## Later
- **Ruby the agent, smarter** — learns what you like and adapts to you.
- **More platforms** — Google Play and beyond, as they clear review.
- **Community-driven content** — player-submitted questions &amp; categories.
