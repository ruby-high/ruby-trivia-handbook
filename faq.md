# FAQ

Straight answers about the app and the token. Utility-first; **targets, not guarantees — nothing here is financial advice or a promise of returns.**

## The app

**What is Ruby Trivia?**
A free trivia + mini-games app for everyone, hosted by **Ruby**, our AI agent. Daily quiz, live multiplayer, practice, friend challenges, leaderboards, streaks — in English, 中文, Español, and Français.

**Is it free? Do I need crypto to play?**
Yes, it's free — no ads, no wallet, no token required. Just open [ruby-trivia.com](https://ruby-trivia.com) and play. The token and wallet features are a separate, optional layer for holders.

**How do I play or install it?**
Open [ruby-trivia.com](https://ruby-trivia.com) in any browser and play today's quiz. It installs to your phone like an app (add to home screen) — no app store needed.

**How does multiplayer work?**
Jump into a live round or challenge a friend head-to-head. Same questions, real time — loser admits the other's smarter.

## The token ($TRIVIA)

**What does the token actually do?**
Holder perks today — a **score multiplier**, a **holders-only leaderboard**, and **cosmetics** — with tournaments and burn-to-mint collectibles on the way. Connect a wallet at [web3.ruby-trivia.com](https://web3.ruby-trivia.com). It's **deflationary** by design: scheduled burns, supply trending down over time.

**Where is it / what chain?**
$TRIVIA is on **Solana**. CA: `CQzNEHjLn3CGVCDet6KFgoxshcCtUFWEfrQe4rTR1V1A`. Always verify the contract address yourself before interacting.

**Is it safe? Are funds locked?**
Mint and LP authority are revoked — supply is fixed at 1B and can only burn down. The dev allocation is locked for 3 months on Streamflow. It's all on chain — go look.

**When price up / when moon?**
Not what we're here for, and not something we'd ever promise — that's the fastest way to lose your trust. We measure ourselves on whether people actually play.

**Is this a pump and dump?**
Fair to ask. The launch was bumpy and the early chart was choppy — we won't pretend otherwise. The difference is there's a real product you can play right now and a team heads-down building it. We're not going anywhere. Judge us over weeks, not minutes.

## Kids + safety

**It's all-ages — isn't a token weird for that?**
The game is for everyone, and kids play **anonymously with no crypto in front of them**. Anything token- or wallet-related is **separate, optional, and adults-only by design.**

## Team + roadmap

**Who's behind it?**
A small crew that ships in the open — more on the [team page](team).

**What's next?**
More games (**Chess** is in the works → Home becomes a mini-game selector), tournaments, app-store listings, and more languages. We give targets, not guarantees, because we'd rather under-promise and ship.
