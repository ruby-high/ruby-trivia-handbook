# How Ruby Trivia works (and who's behind it)

A small, heads-down crew building Ruby Trivia in the open. Here's how we work and who does what.

## The crew
- **Dutch (iono) & Odi** — the two-person core, basically tied across everything: product, the app you play, the backend, the game engine, infrastructure, the token side, and the roadmap. Whatever needs doing, one of them is on it.
- **Destiny** — community + Telegram.
- **mdm** — testing + keeping us honest.
- **Consultants & outside contacts** — market, listings, and app-store guidance (advice, not running the show).

## How we coordinate
- **One source of truth** — decisions made in the open and talked through together.
- **Build in public** — proof on chain for anything we claim.
- We'd rather **ship something simple that works** than promise something complicated that doesn't.

## What we won't do
- **No promises of returns.** It's a utility token — targets, not guarantees, and nothing here is financial advice.
- **No gambling or off-ramp gimmicks.** Kid play stays anonymous and crypto-free; anything token/wallet is optional and adults-only.
- **No rug.** The plan is to build.

## Want in?
Play at [ruby-trivia.com](https://ruby-trivia.com), challenge a friend, hop into our [Telegram](https://t.me/rubytrivia), and tell us what you'd like to see. The best contributors usually start as players.
