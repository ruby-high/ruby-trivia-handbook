## Run of show (each block ≈ 8–12 min → fills ~75 min with Q&A)

---

### 1 · 📱 We're in the Google Play Store  ← **lead with this**
The headline. Months of groundwork — the LLC, the D-U-N-S, the crypto-free store build — and it's a real listing you can install.

- **Free, no ads, no account.** Open it and play. Nothing to sign up for, nothing to buy, kid-safe.
- **Same game everywhere** — the store build is the exact web game, just packaged. Web, Android, all one thing.
- **Why it took real work:** a store build has to be provably clean — we strip anything crypto-facing so the app that ships to families is just the game. That's a feature, not a compromise.
- **Amazon** is in the same queue; **Steam** is next on deck.

🔑 *"Last time this was a promise. Tonight it's a download."*

Audience seed: *"Anyone install it already? What'd you play first?"*

---

### 2 · 🎮 Five new games — live now
Ruby stopped being just a quiz. In the last stretch we shipped a whole shelf:

- **Chess**, **Checkers**, **Sudoku**, **Hangman**, **Math Sprint** — all live.
- These are **real, server-run games**, not reskins — the same engine that runs live multiplayer trivia now runs a board.
- Ruby's becoming a **games hub**, not a one-trick quiz app. Daily quiz and live multiplayer stay the free core; the shelf around them keeps growing.

🔑 *"We went from 'a quiz app' to 'game night' in one stretch."*

Audience seed: *"What game should Ruby add next? We're taking requests."*

---

### 3 · 💎 Why holders care — the library is the perk
*(Framing note: this is the **direction**. Say "the plan," "holders first in line." The recognition layer below IS live; holders-unlock-the-games is a target, not shipped. Do not imply price or return — access only.)*

- The recognition layer is **live today**: verified **holder badge**, **holders-only leaderboard**, **cosmetics** — status and recognition, never gameplay power.
- The direction: **every game Ruby builds is one more thing in the holder library.** As the shelf grows, holding is how you get the full run of it — access, not yield.
- Core stays free for everyone — daily quiz + live multiplayer. Holding is about getting **more to do**, not a leg up on the leaderboard.

🔑 *"Recognition first, always. The perk is more to play — never a better score."*

Say/don't: ✅ "more to unlock," "holders first in line," "recognition-first." ❌ price, returns, "hold and it goes up," "hold to win."

---

### 4 · 🎉 Ruby's becoming a party-game host — Mad Libs, then Incoherent
This is the fun arc. Ruby already writes and vets her own trivia questions autonomously — now she's learning **party games meant to be played out loud, together.**

- **Mad Libs — coming.** Ruby writes fill-in-the-blank stories: everyone shouts words, then she reads it back. The **writing engine is built** — Ruby drafts, self-checks the structure, and a second judge signs off before anything's playable. The game layer's next.
- **Incoherent, family edition — after that.** The read-it-aloud-and-decode-the-gibberish game, kept clean for all ages.
- The thread: Ruby's turning into the **host of the room** — read-aloud, together, family-safe. That's a different lane than solo quizzing, and it's where a lively AI character actually shines.

🔑 *"Ruby didn't just learn trivia. She's learning to run the party."*

Audience seed: *"Who here grew up on Mad Libs? Ruby's about to write infinite ones."*

---

### 5 · 🧭 Where you'll find Ruby next — distribution everywhere
The strategy in one line: **meet players where they already are.**

- **Google Play** — in. ✅
- **Amazon Appstore** — submitted, in review.
- **Steam** — on deck.
- **Seeker dApp Store (Solana Mobile)** — coming. Solana-native store, **zero platform fees**, exactly the right home for a Solana project. This one's on-brand: our token's chain, our store.
- **Discord** — activity coming, so you can play Ruby **inside** a server without leaving chat.

🔑 *"Everywhere people already are — the web, their phone, their Discord, their Solana phone."*

---

### 6 · 🛠️ Why we do it in public
Our whole posture, and the strongest thing we've got:

- **A real product, not a chart.** Everything above is playable or shipping, not a slide.
- **Utility-first.** The token earns its place through recognition and access, not promises about price.
- **Receipts, weekly.** We ship, we say what we shipped, we do these Spaces. Judge us by the log.

🔑 *"Most projects show you a roadmap. We show you a download link."*

---

### 7 · 🙋 Q&A (leave ~15 min)
Prompts to pull people up, and answers to have ready:

- **"When do holders get the games?"** → It's the direction; recognition perks are live now, the game-library perk is on the roadmap. Targets, not dates we'll miss on air.
- **"Is it really free?"** → Yes — free, no ads, no account, kid-safe. That's the store build.
- **"What's the token actually for?"** → Recognition and access. Badge, holders' board, cosmetics today; more to unlock as the library grows. Not a price play.
- **"Android's in — when iOS?"** → Amazon's in review, Steam's next; we go where the review queues let us, one at a time.
- **"How does Ruby write games without going off the rails?"** → She drafts, runs an automatic structural check, then a second AI judge signs off — nothing ships to players unchecked. A human can always overrule.
