# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: June 2026*

This Privacy Policy explains how Ruby Trivia handles your information. It is written to be clear; the Terms of Use cover the legal use of the Service.

## 1. Who we are

Ruby Trivia is operated by Ruby Labs ("Ruby Labs", "we", "us", "our"), the team behind the Ruby family of products from Ruby Labs. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use Ruby Trivia (the "Service"), why we collect it, how we use and share it, and the choices and rights you have.

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at support@ruby-trivia.com.

## 2. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information, depending on how you use the Service:

- Account information — for email accounts: your email address, a password (stored only as a salted hash), and a display name. For kid accounts: a username and password only (no email required).
- Gameplay information — your scores, streaks, XP, level, gems, badges, daily-quiz progress, answers, and the games and topics you play.
- Social information — your friend code, friends you add, challenges you send or accept, and activity shown in community feeds.
- Device and technical information — a device or browser fingerprint used to limit abuse, IP address, browser/OS type, language/locale, and general usage and performance analytics.
- Communications — feedback, bug reports, and support messages you send us.

## 3. How we use information

We use personal information to:

- Provide the Service — run quizzes and live matches, save your progress, and show leaderboards and community activity.
- Operate matchmaking, friend challenges, and social features.
- Keep the Service safe — detect and prevent cheating, abuse, fraud, and violations of our Terms, including via the device fingerprint.
- Provide support and respond to your requests.
- Understand and improve the Service through analytics and aggregated metrics.
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

## 4. Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we process personal data on these legal bases: performance of our contract with you (to provide the Service); our legitimate interests (to secure, support, and improve the Service); your consent (where required, e.g. certain analytics); and compliance with legal obligations. You may withdraw consent at any time.

## 5. Children and families

Ruby Trivia's quiz product is designed to be family-friendly. We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent rules.

- Kid accounts use a username instead of an email, and we minimise the data we collect from them.
- We do not serve targeted/behavioural advertising to children and do not knowingly share children’s personal information with third parties for their own marketing.
- We avoid publicly exposing identifiable information about minors (for example, on public leaderboards) and review content shown in kid-facing features.
- Parents/guardians may review, request deletion of, or refuse further collection of their child’s information by contacting support@ruby-trivia.com. We may need to verify your relationship to the child.
- The Service does not currently offer token, wallet, prize-pool, or other financial features. If we introduce them in the future, they will be adults-only and kept separate from children’s accounts (see the Terms of Use).

## 6. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not "share" it for cross-context behavioural advertising as those terms are defined under California law. We disclose information only:

- To service providers and processors who host our infrastructure, run analytics, or help us operate the Service, under contracts that limit their use of the information to our instructions.
- For legal and safety reasons — to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, or protect the rights, property, and safety of our users, the public, or us.
- In a business transfer — if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.
- In aggregated or de-identified form that cannot reasonably identify you.

## 7. Cookies and local storage

We use browser local storage and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your theme and language, store the anti-abuse device fingerprint, and run essential and analytics functions. You can clear local storage in your browser settings, though doing so may sign you out and reset preferences.

## 8. Future blockchain features

The current Service does not include any blockchain, token, or wallet functionality, and we do not collect wallet addresses or on-chain data. If we add optional blockchain features in the future, they will be adults-only and covered by updated terms and an updated version of this Policy. Note that any information later recorded on a public blockchain would be permanent and could not be changed or deleted by us.

## 9. Data retention

We keep account and gameplay data while your account is active and as needed to provide the Service, then delete or de-identify it within a reasonable period after account closure, unless a longer period is required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes. We minimise retention for children’s data. Aggregated and de-identified analytics may be kept indefinitely.

## 10. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information; to opt out of sale or sharing (we do not sell or share); to limit use of sensitive information; and to be free from discrimination for exercising your rights. EEA/UK users also have the right to object to or restrict processing and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any right, email support@ruby-trivia.com. We will verify your request and respond within the timeframes required by applicable law. You may use an authorised agent where the law allows.

## 11. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organisational safeguards designed to protect personal information, including hashing passwords and limiting access. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your password, and any wallet keys or seed phrase, confidential.

## 12. International transfers

We may process and store information in countries other than where you live, including the United States. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) for international transfers.

## 13. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date and, for material changes, provide additional notice. Your continued use of the Service after changes take effect means you accept the updated Policy.

## 14. Contact us

Privacy questions or requests: support@ruby-trivia.com. General inquiries: hello@ruby-trivia.com.
