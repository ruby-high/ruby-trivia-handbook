# Posting playbook

How we keep a steady drumbeat without burning anyone out.

## Cadence
- Aim for **~1 post every 5 hours**.
- Busy day? **Pre-write 2** and schedule them (8am / 1pm / 7pm ET) — you don't need to be online.
- Goal: a [pile](posts) deep enough that posting runs on autopilot.

## Who
- **iono** stacks the pile. **Everyone knocks 'em down** — grab → post → tick off.
- **MDM** helps carry X (memes + posts).

## Voice
- **Utility-first** — we built a game, not a chart. **No price talk.**
- **No obvious AI copy** — it reads as GPT and people notice. Make it yours.
- Short and human.

## Timing the big stuff
- Hold app-store / listing announcements until the **community's ready to push together** — line up the posts, then drop.
- **Record every Space.**
